Recreation park on the shore of Lake Ontario

01place
Relax in the cozy wooden houses with a fireplace, sauna and Jacuzzi located on the lake shore. For those who like complete privacy, there are houses in the depths of the park, surrounded by ancient trees.
The park has a basketball court, a private beach with a volleyball net, mountain bike and boat rentals. And in winter, you can rent skis or snowmobiles and ride along specially equipped trails. We create an individual entertainment program for each visitor and help to implement it.
There is a playground near each cottage with swings and slides, and there is a children's room on the territory of the park. You will be able to afford any rest knowing that your child is under the supervision of experienced specialists.
We offer not only cozy homes, a wide range of entertainment and activities for you and your family, but also large-scale holidays, sports tournaments and the opportunity to meet new friends.
Despite the remoteness from populated areas, you will not feel disconnected from civilization. All our homes are equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi hotspots and home theaters with a Netflix subscription.
Come to us for a month, all summer or winter — there are good discounts for long-term rentals! Book a house right now, it's enough to order a call, and you can cancel your reservation at any time for free.
